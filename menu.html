Dal Makhani

Fish Curry

Mutton Korma

Aloo Gobi

Idli Sambar

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a mascarpone cheese mixture

Baklava

Baklava is a sweet pastry made of layers of phyllo dough, filled with chopped nuts (often walnuts or pistachios), and sweetened with honey or syrup.

Churros

Churros are deep-fried dough pastries, typically served as long, ridged sticks. They are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside,

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert made from milk solids (khoya) that are deep-fried until golden brown and then soaked in a sweet sugar syrup

Pastel de Nata

Pastel de Nata, also known as Portuguese custard tart, is a pastry filled with a creamy custard and baked until it has a flaky, caramelized top.

Knafeh

Knafeh is a Middle Eastern dessert made from shredded phyllo dough layered with sweet cheese or semolina, soaked in a rosewater and baked until golden and crispy.

Chè

Chè is a Vietnamese dessert soup that comes in various flavors and combinations. It typically includes ingredients like mung beans.

Black Forest Cake

Black Forest Cake, or Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte, is a German dessert consisting of layers of chocolate sponge cake filled with whipped cream and cherries.

Mochi Ice Cream

Mochi ice cream is a Japanese dessert consisting of small, round rice cakes (mochi) filled with ice cream.

Crème Brûlée

Crème brûlée is a French dessert consisting of a rich custard base topped with a layer of caramelized sugar.

Bingsu

Bingsu is a Korean dessert that typically features shaved ice topped with various sweet toppings like condensed milk, fruit, red bean paste, and ice cream.

Tres Leches Cake

Tres leches cake is a moist sponge cake soaked in a mixture of three milks: evaporated milk, condensed milk, and heavy cream.
